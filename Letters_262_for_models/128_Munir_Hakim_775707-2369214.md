I am a retail investor and quarterly reporting is very important to me. I don't think semiannual reporting will have any real benefit to the public at large but could certainly benefit insiders and could lead to more fraud going undetected for longer. Imagine if we made it optional for auto manufactures to put seatbelts in their cars. We all know how that would go. If all the big players change to semiannual reporting, of course the smaller players will follow suit and become the standard for the whole industry. I hope to retire one day with the money I am putting away now. This could have a negative impact on that future. Here in Texas we have a saying, if it ain't broke don't fix it. It doesn't apply to everything but it does here.
