As an investor and former strategic planning executive, understanding the financial position of any company is paramount to successful investing. Reducing the frequency of reported financial details allows business to manage its data to cover potential issues. In addition, quarterly reporting allows any company to present the market with positive performance data or rational explanations for changing market conditions on an ongoing basis. In my opinion, reducing the frequency of reporting is a bad idea.
