From: Anthony Sirica

I think this option should be structured like Section 404(b) SOX compliance. It clearly should be available to SRCs as most investors in these size companies seem to focus solely on the earnings releases. It should not be available to large cap companies (however that is defined for these purposes). As a CPA and investor in many companies like AAPL, NVDA, BXSL, ARES, JPM, MOB, etc., I obtain a lot of information from the 10-Qs that is not in the earnings releases. I couldn't imagine getting that information twice a year, especially in the post-COVID capital markets. Take Apple for example. The latest earnings release has 4 small paragraphs about their quarterly results.
