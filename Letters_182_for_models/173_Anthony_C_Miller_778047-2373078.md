Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Anthony C Miller

Absolutely under no circumstance is it better for investors institutional or retail to have less insight or information on the companies which they are choosing to invest in. And with current political climates in mind it seems that such a ruling would allow companies to obscure or downplay risks and play into or spin positively any possible risks which may be negative for said companies to disclose. With that in mind it would seem to increase volatility around those periods of reporting rather than a drip or consistent updates periodically. Long story short more information = better for investors
