Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: David Bach

Sir or Madam: Two reasons to avoid semi-annual reporting. (1) Less transparency leads to more uncertainly, which leads to less trading and greater volatility. This increased risk will lower stock prices. That upsets investors, leading to further less trading. The downward cycle continues. (2) Companies should not bite the hand that feeds them. Upset investors may not support the companies' future funding needs.
