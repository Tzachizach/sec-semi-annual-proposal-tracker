Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Michael Shore

Under NO conditions should companies be allowed to only report every 6 months instead of quarterly. History, reaching back centuries, is full of companies acting illegally; the only way that behavior comes to light is through their quarterly reporting (and not always then, if they falsify financial records). Leaving companies to only report every 6 months would greatly increase the potential harm to investors with the corresponding damage to the stock markets and the US economy. It is best to catch such illegal behavior earlier, while it's a smaller problem, instead of giving companies more time to create a catastrophe. Protect the markets!
