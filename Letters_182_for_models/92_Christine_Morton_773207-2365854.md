Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Christine Morton

I am against semi-annual reporting for public companies and prefer that quarterly reporting continues. We have enough challenges with transparency as it is. I appreciate it's a reporting burden for public companies, but how are investors supposed to hold boards accountable without timely reporting? We need transparency. Semi-annual reporting is too late in many cases if companies pursue activities against their investors' best interests.

I am a US citizen residing in the UK and this proposed rule is not in the interest of market transparency. It will affect the US's standing as a global market. Global investors may pull money out of US markets if they do not feel their investments are safe, and one way to determine that is via quarterly, regular reporting.
