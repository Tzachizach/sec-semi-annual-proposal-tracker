Subject: S7-2026-15 — Webform Comments from Patrick Ryan
From: Patrick Ryan

This policy is meant to alleviate a small regulatory burden that all affected companies are well-equipped to shoulder.

The quarterly reporting system has been a staple of American financial trust and safety. The downside and avenues for insider trading are clear and likely, while the upsides are small and concentrated in the hands of a few thousand publicly traded corporations.

I urge you to reconsider and terminate this proposal, on behalf of the average American investor and general public.
