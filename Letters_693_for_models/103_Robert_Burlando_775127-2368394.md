Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Robert Burlando

As an individual investor, I do not support the idea of allowing public companies listed on US exchanges to switch from quarterly to semiannual reporting. Proponents of semiannual reporting point to the practice in Europe but it is my perception that semiannual reporting there has hidden corporate malfeasance that may have been caught earlier had companies been required to report quarterly. Further, I believe US capital markets are the most transparent and liquid in the world, and quarterly reporting plays an important part in maintaining our global leadership in this critical area.
Thank you for your consideration.
