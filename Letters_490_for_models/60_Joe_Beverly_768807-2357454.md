Subject: File No. S7-2026-15

I would be opposed to such a change.

Any company, whether public or private, should be preparing and reviewing monthly financial statements. If monthly statements are being prepared, with modern accounting systems, it is not difficult to print quarterly statements.

I admit there is more work to be done than just printing the statements, but I believe investors in public companies should not have to wait for six months to get an update from a public company.
