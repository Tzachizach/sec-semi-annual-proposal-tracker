Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: William Keisler

I believe that changing the mandatory corporate filings from 3 months to 6 months will be a mistake. Such a move will reduce corporate transparency at a time when such a thing already feels low. Less frequent updates will erode investor confidence, and will outweigh the marginal savings companies may find from this proposed change.
