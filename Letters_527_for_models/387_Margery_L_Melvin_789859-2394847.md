Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Margery L. Melvin

I do not support changing corporate reporting requirements from quarterly to biannual reporting.  Biannual reporting is not often enough for individual investors to be fully apprised of a company's financial health.  Not being fully apprised of a company's financial health would seriously limit opportunities for investors like me to invest.  I do not support this and ask the SEC to maintain quarterly reporting requirements for all companies on the exchange.
