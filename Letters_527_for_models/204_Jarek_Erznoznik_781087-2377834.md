I think semi annuall reporting is a poor idea. It keeps companies less responsible to the public retail investors that also are included on the market. More opportunity for fraud and corruption to take place when there is more time and less pressure from the public on large public corporations. These are public companies and their numbers should be PUBLIC accordingly. Public reporting is not an afterthought. This is a bad excuse to do less work and be less responsible to the public.
