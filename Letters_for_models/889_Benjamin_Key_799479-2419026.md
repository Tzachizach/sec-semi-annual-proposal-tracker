Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Benjamin Key

Quarterly reports are a basic duty to shareholders so that we can make informed decisions on how to invest in our future. Whether that be multi-billion dollar hedge funds or small individual investors, everyone relies on these reports. This would also effect the overall economy as without regular reports, companies would go over or undervalued for longer periods of time, causing more dramatic swings in the market when the proposed semiannual reports finally go out.
