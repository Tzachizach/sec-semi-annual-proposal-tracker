Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Blake Webb

I am here to publicly voice my opinion AGAINST this rule change. I believe lowering the requirement for minimum finance reports/guidance between annual reporting from 3 to 1 will introduce more speculation into the stock market. It would hurt retail investors the most. As a retail investor, I would feel greatly uninspired to invest in a stock market where it has become a trend to only provide 1 report between annual reporting. I would constantly be worried about my 401k investments because I would not want to be invested in assets that refuse to be as transparent.
