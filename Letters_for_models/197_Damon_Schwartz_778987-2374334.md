Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Damon Schwartz

I rely on quarterly information to make decisions, longer gaps create an unfair advantage for corporate insiders and would increase the presence and likelihood of insider trading.

The "optional" statement doesn't fix the problem because companies that opt in are often the ones whose investors most need frequent updates, the savings to companies aren't worth the cost to me as an individual.

Please announce 6 benefits of such changes from a perspective of 5-10 years down the line, and provide the direct benefits to investors, and the potential benefits that will be present as a byproduct of such a change.

This does not affect my viewpoint, what it does is provide clarity for such a decision to be made.
