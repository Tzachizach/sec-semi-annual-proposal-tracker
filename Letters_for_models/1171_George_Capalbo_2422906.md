My name is George from Massachusetts. I am a proud US citizen. 

I have benefitted greatly from the stock market for a long time. Rules that make it harder to cook the books have kept me safe, and have also generally kept many other investors like myself safe. 

I am VERY concerned with this change. If the rest of the world is walking back their changes AFTER trying it, then why are we going ahead with it? 

Bad. Very bad idea. 

We're supposed to be leading in investor confidence - not creating the conditions to make insider trading easier. 

More transparency is better. 

This change would materially harm not just me, but also (in my opinion) the national security of our country in the long run. 

Bad business leads to less investment overall, in a time where we need all the advantages we can get.
