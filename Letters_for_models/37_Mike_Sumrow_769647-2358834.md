# Letter 37 — Mike Sumrow

- **Date:** 2026-05-06
- **Source:** https://www.sec.gov/comments/S7-2026-15/s7202615-769647-2358834.html

---

I strongly oppose the proposed SEC rule change to reduce public company reporting requirements from quarterly to semiannually. As a private investor, I insist on transparency of companies through quarterly fiscal reporting. The burden of quarterly reporting is small today, in this age of computers and business software. I will check my investments and sell any company that adopts the semiannual reporting rule, should the SEC establish such a rule.
