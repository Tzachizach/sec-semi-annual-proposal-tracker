Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Walter

Reporting requirements are going the wrong direction. 

The internet and technology should allow investors to get live reporting information daily (we should be able to see what's underneath a stock price immediately (and for free)). This would allow for greater performance transparency, efficient markets, price stability and investment democratization, and increased healthy competition between companies. 

Companies already have live data reporting dashboards. Loosen the legal requirements and allow companies to publish easy to understand metrics. 

Hiding information feels very third world, and antiquated. It feels like regressing from AI to the printing press.
