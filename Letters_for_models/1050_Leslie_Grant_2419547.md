Leslie Grant, Financial products wholesaler. 

While I deal with investments and investment products day in and day out, I am not an insider. I rely on the free flow of publicly available information to make informed investment decisions about my own portfolio and to guide the financial advisors whom I serve. 

By reducing the required reporting to bi-annual, we as every day investors do not get access to the data we need to make informed decisions about the fiscal health of the companies with whom we invest. 

The world and markets move in real time, with markets trading virtually 24/7 the lack of timely information greatly disadvantages retail investors allowing insiders and institutional investors to dump their shares while those of us in retail are buying on outdated information. 

This rule does not protect investors, it allows companies to hide and institutions to profit on the backs of every day people just like the misguided SpaceX/NASDAQ index addition.
