Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Bernard Zhang

Although I understand the reasoning behind this proposal, this is a terrible idea for transparency. Most of the companies will be fine, but companies that are already a bit more on the fringe/smaller will be able to push back any poor performance or fraud for longer before anything gets discovered. Any companies committing potential fraud would be able to get away with it for longer, potentially magnifying the degree of loss when it occurs.
