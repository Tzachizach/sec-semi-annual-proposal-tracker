Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Richard Norelli

Do NOT reduce corporate filing requirements to semi-annually.  As an individual who needs information to protect my retirement funds, our family benefits from access to current information about the fiscal health of the firms involved in our invested savings.  Reducing that period only benefits corporate manipulation and privileged executives.  Speaking as the "small guy" this rule revision reflects corporate/government cronyism and does very little to reduce costs.  I don't have powerful attorneys or lobbyists at my disposal.  I rely on the fairness of the SEC.
