Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Ellen Harvey

Reducing the number of times public companies report earnings reduces the transparency investors have into those companies. Companies move fast and changes in their earnings and strategies needs to be visible. For example, Allbirds sold their shoe brand and pivoted from sustainable shoes to artificial intelligence. Without quarterly information investors will have no idea what is going on.
