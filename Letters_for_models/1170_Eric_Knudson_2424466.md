Quarterly reporting serves as a valuable information tool for investors. Reducing the frequency of accurate disclosures will increase fraud and exacerbate the role rumor, misinformation, and fear play in destabilizing securities markets. Ultimately, the proposed rule change would make regulatory enforcement more difficult; undermine the ability of investors to assess the performance and quality of management; and reduce the quality, frequency, and timeliness of the information available to investors.
The proposed rule change should not be adopted.
