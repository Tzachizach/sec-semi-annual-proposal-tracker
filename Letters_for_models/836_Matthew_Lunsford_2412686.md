I'm retiring in August. Moving the reporting period for companies from 3 months to 6, does not benefit the investment decisions of my pension fund or myself. It does give a big advantage to those inside the company whether a company upturn or downturn occurs. Advantages listed as part of the push for this are not borne out when you look at other nations around the world. Many who have tried it appear to be retreating. In conclusion, this appears to be a bad idea. 
Matthew Lunsford
