# Letter 150 — Edward Dennison

- **Date:** 2026-05-14
- **Source:** https://www.sec.gov/comments/S7-2026-15/s7202615-777187-2372014.html

---

Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Edward Dennison

I strongly oppose this proposed rule. Current reporting requirements provide excellent visibility for investors into the conditions and prospects of publicly traded companies.

There has been no evidence presented that the cost of biannual as opposed to quarterly reporting is materially different.

The United States leads the world in the transparency, accessibility, and depth of its capital markets. This proposed rule can only erode those qualities.
