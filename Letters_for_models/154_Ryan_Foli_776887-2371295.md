Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Ryan Foli

The new SEC rule being proposed is a terrible idea. Do not do it.

If this rule becomes reality, the stock market will be much worse off. If there are only 2 reports instead of 4, every report wil be that much more volatile. Every earnings report will be more volatile, and the market will be a more dangerous place to invest. I can see it now. Concerning geopolitics and business - business changes a lot, and only giving investors 2 reports a year is only going to make investing worse.

What sense does it make to reduce company reporting, to reduce the visibility of operations and allow companies to hide from its owners, its investors? Reducing visibility is never a good thing in the financial world, and this proposal stinks! Please. Do not do it.
