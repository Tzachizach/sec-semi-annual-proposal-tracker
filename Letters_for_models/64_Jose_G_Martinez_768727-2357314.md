Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Jose G. Martinez

As a private investor, I strongly prefer that public companies continue reporting earnings quarterly rather than semiannually. Quarterly reports provide timely transparency, help investors make informed decisions, and maintain accountability in the markets. Reducing the frequency of reporting would limit access to current financial information and could increase uncertainty for individual investors who rely on regular updates to evaluate company performance.
