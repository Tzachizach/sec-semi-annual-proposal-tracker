Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Fresnel Phan

The US Securities Market is easily the largest such market in the world. We are the market of choice for the largest companies in the world and receive capital flows from the entire globe. I cannot think of any reason why we would ruin what has made our markets so successful and the global standard of transparency and stability. Other country's markets that have moved to semiannual reporting are not as large and successful as the US market and I have seen no evidence that moving to semiannual reporting provided any positive benefit to their markets. These smaller markets should not be providing any inspiration for the rules that apply to our own market.
