I write in opposition to the proposed reduction in reporting frequency. Quarterly reporting protects retail investors. I listen to the quarterly earnings calls and read many of the reports for the clients I'm a shareholder in and it helps inform me about my investments. I would prefer not to have less visibility into the companies I owe if others will be able to know and trade on that non public information. 

If you do this then all controlled officer's shares should not be tradeable for any reason outside of a 7 day window immediately following the public earnings release. 

- H. Michael Wayland
