Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Cyrus James Stombaugh

I oppose the proposal to switch from quarterly to semiannual reporting. Six months is too long for investors to go without updated financial information, especially when a company's situation can change quickly. Reducing paperwork for companies shouldn't come at the cost of transparency. Keeping quarterly reports helps protect everyday investors and keeps markets informed.
