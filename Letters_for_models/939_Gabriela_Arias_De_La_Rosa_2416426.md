To whom it might concern:

I hope you reconsider this change to the rules. It would harm both companies and investors. By making it easier for companies to withhold relevant financial information, it could reduce investor confidence and make them less inclined to invest, ultimately lowering company valuations.

Personally, I would sell any company in my portfolio that chose to report only twice a year rather than quarterly.
