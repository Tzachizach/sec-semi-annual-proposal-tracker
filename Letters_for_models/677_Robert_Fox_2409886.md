My name is Robert Fox and I am an Electrical Engineer. I believe that quarterly reporting is good for market stability over the long term. Semi-annual reporting seems to only serve those with insider knowledge, allowing retail investors to be taken advantage of. A change like this would also serve to reduce overall confidence in publicly traded stocks and could lead to far less market participation. Please keep quarterly reporting requirements.
