Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: John & Lynn Ingewrsen

My wife and I feel strongly that the discontinuation of quarterly reports will be a disservice to those of us who have been investing for years and owe our comfortable retirement to being able to see what a company is doing on an ongoing basis. I feel this so strongly that I have spent the last 35 years teaching (at no cost to the student) classes on how to invest without a broker, how to understand and read the quarterly report, how interpret it to avoid mistakes. Value Line is an invaluable resource that depends heavily on the quarterly report as I do. Please continue the reports requirement as it now exists!
