Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Alex Matache

The foundational principle which guides the success of the American Financial System is trust. This trust is born from the transparency and rigor by which we hold our institutions accountable. If we allow this transparency to erode, we invite the kind of dishonesty and deceit that will be the eventual unraveling of this system.

Market participants need to be truthful and forth-bearing in their economic activities. They need to be so in a timely manner. Quarterly earnings have been an accurate and acceptable barometer for over 50 years. There is no reason to change this. The only parties that benefit from this are insiders, manipulators, and unscrupulous groups who feel the need to hide their wrongdoings.

Keep the markets fair and transparent for the public. This is in the best interest of this country and its future prospective economic growth.

To those that wish to pursue less regulated business ventures, there is always private equity. Keep the markets fair and transparent for those of us who wish to invest in the future in this country with trust and honesty. Keep reports quarterly. Its in the best interests of everyone.
