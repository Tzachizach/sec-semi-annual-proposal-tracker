Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: David C.

I'm a retail investor and I'm concerned that this change will materially and negatively affect my ability to invest with adequate information. As a retail investor, I do not have the resources that big firms do to obtain as much data. When changing to less frequent reporting, we will end up having to rely more on "rumors" without companies clearing up rumors as frequently. It is cited that this change will motivate companies to "focus more on the long term plan for the company,"" however, that is already a choice present to every company, it is up to the company what they focus on. Additionally, it's far from guaranteed that companies will shift their mindset. I request that this be reconsidered.
