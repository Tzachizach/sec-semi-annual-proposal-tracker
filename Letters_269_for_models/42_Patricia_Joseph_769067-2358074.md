That quarterly reporting takes time away from managing a business is NOT TRUE. In my experience in senior management roles in ops and finance at Fortune 500 companies, quarterly reporting enhances performance. The process of preparing for quarterly reports communicates what's really going on up and down the chain of command and of promotes accountability. Like early warning system if there are problems. Twice a year? A lot of bad things can happen in 6 months.
