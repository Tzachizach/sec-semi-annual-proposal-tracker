Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Erin McBrien

I am independent investor who uses the quarterly reports to guide my investing decisions. As a successful, independent investor, I do not have access to all of the information that an institutional investment firm such as Fidelity might be able to access, so I rely on these SEC filings. I regularly use the 10-Q to provide a complete picture of the financial health of a company. Moving to semi-annual requirement will unfairly penalize individual investors as we will not have access to the same timely information as institutional investors.
