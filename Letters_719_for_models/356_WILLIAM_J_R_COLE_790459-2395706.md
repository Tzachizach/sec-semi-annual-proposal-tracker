From: WILLIAM J. R. COLE

File Number S7-2026-15
Gentlemen:  I encourage you to keep the current quarterly SEC filing requirements for all publicly traded companies, and NOT to reduce such filing requirements to a semi-annual basis.  Protecting the public's interests in audited, fairly-stated financial reports on a quarterly basis allows for discovery of any fraudulent financial activities more quickly and gives myriad individual investors the opportunity to review a company's financial health more often.  Thank you for considering my comments. 
William J. R. Cole, Individual Investor and Lifetime Member of Better Investing.
