Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Richard Rinvelt

I am an individual investor and use the quarterly reports extensively. My concern is that many smaller companies will not report quarterly because of this proposed change in reporting.
This will put many of the individual investors at a disadvantage. We use these quarterly reports as a means to see the progress of the small and medium size companies that we invest in. 
If I don't have current  information ( Quarterly reports) I will not be investing as much as I am now because I will not have current and up to date information on the company.
