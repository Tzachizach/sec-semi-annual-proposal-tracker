Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Grace Montgomery

I have been an investor for over 25 years.  I have both private accounts with two brokerages, 401K and 403(B) accounts.  I read the reports that a company generates, files and sends out to gather information and, make informed decisions on purchasing or selling a holding.  
If the required reports are reduced the public as well as the investment firms are then making uninformed decisions.  A company could suddenly be having problems and, with the reduced reporting we (myself, the public and investment firms) will be unaware of greatly reduced earnings, increased debt and impending doom.

Please keep the reporting requirements as they are so we all can make better informed decisions.

Regards,
Grace Montgomery
