# Letter 548 — Better Investing

- **Date:** 2026-05-28

Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Better Investing

Investors want/need quarterly reports to help them manage their investments.  Most financial information comes monthly (banking, etc)......investments are the exception.  Making them only twice a year will make it more difficult for investors to track important financial information.
