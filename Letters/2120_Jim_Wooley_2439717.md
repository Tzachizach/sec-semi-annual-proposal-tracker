Jun. 7, 2026

 
My wife and I have been actively investing in the stock market throughout the last 52 years. We are "regular folks", not deep-pocket investors, who have done well in the market under the regulatory rules you propose to change. 

We have relied through that half century on your oversight of companies. We appreciate and value the information that quarterly reporting by companies provides to our portfolio managers for the varied financial instruments that now comprise our retirement portfolio. We believe it is critical that this oversight continue. 

We respectfully request that quarterly reports continue to be required for companies that are a part of the various U.S. Stock Exchanges. Thank you. 

James & Amy Wooley, Retired Biologist and Teacher.
