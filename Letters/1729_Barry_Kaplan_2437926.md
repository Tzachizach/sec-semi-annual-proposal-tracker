To the SEC;

I object to changing the corporate reporting requirements from 3 to 6 months. This will make companies’ financial transparency more difficult to follow and not help retail investors make better informed investing decisions.

If your goal is to reduce or eliminate what little trust is left in public disclosure, go ahead, this is not what taxpayer citizens expect from their government.
