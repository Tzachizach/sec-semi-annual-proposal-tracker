Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: ELIZABETH BOLINT

Working as in house counsel over many years, in several different major corporations, I have seen the human temptation of number manipulation to be very strong - because bonuses, salary and promotions are conditional on meeting certain numbers... not quality or customer satisfaction, which are harder factors to evaluate. It is a human weakness. Quarterly reporting discourages this weakness. It is better to have mechanisms that support honesty for the public, and investors, and help corporations stay fair and honest. Like traffic laws... the SEC Quarterly reporting is a mechanism that unconsciously keep life orderly and fair.
