Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Jonathan Hains

This is a very bad idea. This rule change will make US capital markets nearly 50% less efficient.
Market efficiency derives from the amount of information that is publicly known to investors.
Those with access to management (bankers, institutional investors, and other forms of private capital) will now benefit even MORE from the unequal access they enjoy to management teams, even after taking into account Reg FD.

With less information, and less efficient markets, equity prices will need to discount a larger risk premium, which translates to lower stock valuations. That makes businesses less able to invest in productivity-enhancing investments and innovation.

In a world where everything goes faster, its a very bad idea to have the flow of financial and market information go slower.

Please vote to reject the proposal.
