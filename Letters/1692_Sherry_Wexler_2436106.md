Dear SEC: 

Allowing businesses to switch to semi-annual reporting instead of quarterly is a bad idea. It is an unfair practice that would keep ordinary investors in the dark while big institutional investors and CEOs would have the advantage of knowing what's going on. 

For me, I'm retirement age though still workung and I have some investments. Seeing a quarterly report is essential. 

There is no real reason to do this except to pander to big business. We need a middle class, do you know? If you keep feeding these wealth divisions, you crush the health of our country. Please do the right thing!
