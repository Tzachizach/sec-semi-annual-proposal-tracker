Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Cheryl Shaw

Many of us private investors rely on 10K reporting and other public disclosures to make informed decisions.  I oppose semi-annual reporting.  Quarterly reporting does not interfere with investment decisions and I hope that decision makers would understand the friction and barriers (like AI and algorithm-driven trading) that are currently reducing, if not blocking, private, unsophisticated investors ability to achieve profits.  Restricting to semi-annual reporting would only make it impossible for a personal investor to make intelligent decisions. 

In the age of AI, why would we not move toward MORE reporting instead of less?
