Jun. 1, 2026

 
I am writing to oppose Rule S7-2026-15. This rule allows for a hostile market environment and threatens the wellbeing of hundreds of thousands of hardworking Americans who rely on their retirement savings to survive and a profitable market to keep those savings aloft. Allowing companies to essentially hide poor performance passes their issues onto the consumer rather than handling it themselves.
