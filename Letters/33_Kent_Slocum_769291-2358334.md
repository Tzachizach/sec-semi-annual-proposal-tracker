# Letter 33 — Kent Slocum

- **Date:** 2026-05-06
- **Role/Affiliation:** Individual
- **Stance:** Oppose
- **Source:** https://www.sec.gov/comments/S7-2026-15/s7202615-769291-2358334.html

---

Modern corporations need more oversight and accountability, not less. Reducing annual reporting requirements for companies from quarterly to biannually will only hurt investors and damage what little goodwill remains between the American public and the corporate sector. US regulators are already seen as extremely partisan and highly corrupt; taking this action will only serve to remove all doubt that the SEC serves business interests, not the interests of ordinary, hard-working, blue-collar Americans.
