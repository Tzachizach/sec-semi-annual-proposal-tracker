Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Philip-Michael Enix

Reduced frequency of information disclosure reduces transparency and increases risk for individual or retail investors who do not have access to the same tools or resources as institutional investors to seek validation of their investment or thesis on the company outlook. I am against allowing any opt-in to an alternative reporting. Companies should be focused on long term strategy regardless.
