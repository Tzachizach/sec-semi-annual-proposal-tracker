Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Melanie Bieder

I disagree strongly with the potential change to business quarterly report filings.  The regular reports have been used for decades and companies have consistent procedures for producing them.  As an investor I have relied on these quarterly reports for three or more decades to evaluate a firm's progress on their initiatives and my assessment to continue to invest in the firm.  Removing the quarterly requirement will mean firms may then have lax control over their finances, etc., allowing them to fudge information or postpone important information to the public.  The quarterly report should stay as it has been required for decades.
