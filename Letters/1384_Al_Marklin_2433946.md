Albert Marklin, IT Manager: 
Quarterly reporting is absolutely essential to maintain investor confidence and protect retail investors against predatory insider trading! The burden on companies is minimal compared to the harm and abuse that would, (WOULD, not "could") be visited on the market. 
A six month interval makes it far too easy to hide malfeasance and damaging financials for investors to make informed decisions. DO NOT ELIMINATE QUARTERLY REPORTING!
