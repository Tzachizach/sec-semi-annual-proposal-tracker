Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Robert Dunning

The proposal for required quarterly financial statements to be changed to semi-annual should be rejected. The potential for inside dealing will substantially increase due to insufficient exposure. Fair market trading will be severely harmed as smaller investors will be making decisions with much less information. The potential for abuse, subsequent turmoil and scandal is far too high.
