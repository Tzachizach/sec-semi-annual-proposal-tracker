Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Oscar L. Perez

Under no circumstances should this move forward. As a retail investor, it is already difficult to access or contact corporate investor relations people at firms for various reasons.

Although it is true that there is some short-term minded incentive to focus on earnings beats every quarter, which hinders the long term, this is a management issue, not an investor issue. If firms do not like the quarterly reporting system, it shows their own short-term focus and bias.

Management sets the tone at the top. If they focus on the quarters and not the years, investors will focus on the quarters, not the years. This is a self-fulfilling prophecy. A firm culture issue, not a systematic issue.

Sincerely,
Oscar L. Perez
