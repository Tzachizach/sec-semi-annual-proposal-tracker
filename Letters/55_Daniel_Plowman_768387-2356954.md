Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Daniel Plowman

I feel like semiannual reporting is a win for regular people. When companies report twice a year, they are not running each quarter with a focus only on earnings. With fewer mandatory reporting periods, leaders can manage their businesses and do what is best for the people they serve rather than serving only shareholders. I invest and really only pay attention to Q2 and Q4 earnings anyway. Those two quarters are centered on the ideal spending seasons, spring and winter. Preparing for summer and Christmas (holiday) shopping.

However, I can see this going both ways, opening up less oversight and, essentially, creating opportunities for fraud. That being said, I still fully support semiannual reporting and feel it will help people who rely on businesses for services or goods rather than having those businesses operate in a manner that does not treat each customer fairly.
