From: Katherine Winfield

This dilutes the purpose of the reports. On the surface it sounds reasonable but a basic examination reveals that semiannual reporting negatively affects those sought to be protected. These reports are prepared for shareholders on a regular basis. It is not a liability to the companies to provide this to the government. Essentially, neither public benefit nor actual company benefit exists with change. The change could allow a period when companies could avoid penalties for disallowed activities. That would, indeed, harm consumers.
