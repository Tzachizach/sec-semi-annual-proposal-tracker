Jun. 11, 2026

 
I strongly believe changing the reporting requirements from quarterly to semiannually will harm the public and encourage and reward public companies to act with bad intent. Please keep the quarterly reporting requirements as a sign of good faith and accountability and stewardship for the general the public. In fact, in the current era of digital and electronic banking, assets, funds and reporting, please consider introducing monthly reporting requirements. With the deployment of AI in every aspect of our lives, this should not be an onerous task. - M. Rafathullah, 
Concerned citizen 
Paralegal.
