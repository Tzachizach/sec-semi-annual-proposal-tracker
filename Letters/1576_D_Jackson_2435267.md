To SEC, 

I am writing to express my strong opposition to any changes regarding the current reporting requirements for publicly traded companies. It is essential that these companies continue to provide quarterly reports, as has been the standard for over 60 years. 

Transparency and truthfulness in quarterly earnings reports are vital for the public. These requirements must remain in place to ensure corporate accountability. 

Best regards, 

Mr. D Jackson
