Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Meera K

I am against the changes allowing companies to make semiannual filings to the public. As an investor with an individual brokerage account relies on quarterly filings to make informed decisions on my investments, and need a smaller gap between filings to ensure the info is relevant and that insiders have less time to gain an advantage. It's important for quarterly filings to stay required and I am also against companies to have the option to file semiannually as this defeats the purpose of quarterly filings.
