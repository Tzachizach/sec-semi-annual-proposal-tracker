Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Christopher Haag

Business performance is a significant anchor is determining the value of a business. Over time business performance is not linear, so routine reporting on a quarterly basis allows individual investors to properly understand business trajectory for companies they are invested in   The SEC has a responsibility to protect the interests of individual investors who invest their retirement savings into these companies and NOT to cater to the lobbying interests of various corporations. Effective companies are able to automate part of this reporting process anyhow, so any complaint of "burden" for quarterly reporting is bogus.
