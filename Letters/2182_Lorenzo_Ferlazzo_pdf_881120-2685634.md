Cascading Market Risks
Information Asymmetry Spikes
Less frequent, non-standardised reporting strips active
investors of timely material data. |
| Liquidity & Valuation Erraticism
Trading costs increase and market liquidity drops. Investors rely on
peer-firm signals, leading to overreactions and severe mispricing.
Stalled Capital Formation
Ironically defeating the SEC's goal, delayed positive reporting
prevents growing firms from leveraging a blowout quarter to
raise external financing at lower capital costs.

Widening the Insider Trading Window
Current Mandatory 10-Q
Rapid public price discovery limits the lifespan of non-public material information.
Proposed 10-S
The 6-Month Runway
Extending the interval between mandatory reports dramatically widens the gap
between inside knowledge and public awareness. Insiders gain a dangerously long
window to act on, and profit from, non-public data before the market catches on.
