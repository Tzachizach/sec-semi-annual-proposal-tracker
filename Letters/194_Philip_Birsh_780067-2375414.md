Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Philip Birsh

This change is a terrible idea. Corporations must act as responsible organizations and inform the public of its results and must explain their results. Investors will be damaged by this terrible idea of semi annual reporting. It will promote more insiders having advantages over normal individuals. It cannot serve the public's interests when a company is allowed to financially spiral downward for half of a year under this cloak of non-reporting. How do we guardians of pension funds protect our people's retirement funds when we have no idea what is happening in the companies we invest in for this amount of time?
No one with any financial oversight responsibilities would ever think this new change is intelligent. It will damage investors and only serve the irresponsible. The Chinese stock market does not require GAAP accounting. This terrible idea is similar in practice. Do not make this change. It will hurt the majority of investors in our capital markets who are not in the top tiers of the investment community. Thank you for reading my position.
