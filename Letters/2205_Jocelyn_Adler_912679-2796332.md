Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Jocelyn Adler

As a professional researcher, I rely on the information filed in quarterly reports to provide information and insights into the companies we do business with, including our clients. By taking away this source of information, the data available will be stale and easier to obfuscate. If this is a problem for a librarian at a midsize law firm, it will be a bigger problem for enforcement agencies and government officials. 


Speaking as a private citizen, I remember what the Enron disaster did to my 401k. These rules were passed for a reason, and have not slowed the historic growth of our economy in the years since.
