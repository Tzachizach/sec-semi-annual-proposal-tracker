Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Demetrius Stavros

The SEC has previously conducted the experiment of reducing the quarterly Form 10-Q reporting requirement to a semi-annual frequency. It is ridiculous to think that the public deserves less information, especially in the current climate. If anything, given the availability of digital record keeping, calculating, and reporting methods, the report requirement should be more frequent than quarterly. At a minimum, do not increase the time between required reports.
