Quarterly financials should be the minimum standard to ensure that public companies disclose material changes in the operations and their ability to continue as a going concern. Changing this to a semi annual event in today’s fast paced world would be a mistake and constitute a dereliction of duty and fiduciary responsibility that falls to officers and boards of directors of public companies. I implore you not to change the system that has served us well for many years.

Respectfully yours,
