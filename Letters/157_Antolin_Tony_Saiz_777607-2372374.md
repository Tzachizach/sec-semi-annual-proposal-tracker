Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Antolin (Tony) Saiz

I would like to ask that the Commission not change the quarterly reporting requirements of public corporations. As a private citizen, investing my own funds and not paying any investment agents for their advice, I find it imperative to be able to access current information on the financial status and income/losses of the companies in which I invest. I have neither the access nor the privilege of speaking directly to the representatives of these companies; I am not able to ask questions or follow up on my doubts on presentations, and so must exclusively rely on the 10-Q for quarterly information and the 10-K for the annual results. Please do not take this accessibility away from small investors, as we have no other recourse to protect our investments.
