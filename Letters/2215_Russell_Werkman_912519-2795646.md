Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Russell Werkman

Dear Committee Members,

I am against the proposal to reduce the number of required corporate reports from four per year to two per year.  

The reporting done by public corporations  is so important to ensure that small investors, like me, have the information we need to make informed decisions.  

While I am aware that the reporting takes time, effort, and money, as a shareholder, I am willing to pay those costs to ensure I know what is going on.  This is especially important in the current world, where information changes so quickly.  

Thank you for your consideration,

Russell Werkman
