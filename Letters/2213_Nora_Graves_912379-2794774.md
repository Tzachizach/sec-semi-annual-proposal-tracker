I am against changing corporate reporting periods from quarterly to semi-annual. 

We see news that events are affecting the United States daily. War, tariffs, blockades, oil shortages, etc., have dramatic effects on all of us, including our US companies. Imagine that a major event occurs July 1, but the effect on the companies isnât known until next January. This is totally unacceptable.

Changing the reporting period means that investors will not have the necessary information to determine where to invest their money. Investors could move their money to other countries, where reporting is more regulated. This does not serve the interests of either corporations or investors.
