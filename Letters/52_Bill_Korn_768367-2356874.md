# Letter 52 — Bill Korn

- **Date:** 2026-05-05
- **Role/Affiliation:** Board Member and Audit Chair, Jerash Holdings (US) Inc.; Board Member and former CFO of CareCloud, Inc.
- **Stance:** Conditional

Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Bill Korn

While I see the appeal for many issuers of only reporting twice a year, investors are better served with quarterly reporting, and the Board can do a more effective job of assessing performance with quarterly reports.

If we allow issuers the option of choosing semi-annual reporting, it would be helpful to relax the timeframe for reporting if they choose to stay with quarterly. I would rather see a quarterly report 60 days after quarter-end then wait for a semiannual report 40 or 45 days after the end of the half. Especially for smaller reporting companies, a little extra time might encourage them to stay with quarterly reports.
