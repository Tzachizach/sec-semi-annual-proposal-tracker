Dear SEC,
I am writing because I feel transparency is important if we are investing our money into a business we want to see thrive. Please do not rob investors of the ability to understand the health of a company each quarter. Allowing companies to report every six months has zero benefit to the investor. It gives companies a free pass to hide information about the health of their business for up to 6 months.

If this were to be passed, it would limit my desire to invest in the US stock market.
