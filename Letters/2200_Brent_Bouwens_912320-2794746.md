Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Brent Bouwens

I am commenting to request the SEC keep the current quarterly reporting requirement as is .  As a retiree I depend on my 401k and other investments, and rely on quarterly reporting to ensure accountability and transparency.  Going back to semi-annual reporting will result in unaccountability and disaster. There is no benefit to the American public in changing the current requirements and regulations.  Thank you for your consideration.
