Re: Subject: S7-2026-15 

To whom it may concern at the SEC, 
I would like to object to the above and as that you redact your proposal allowing listed companies to opt-out of quarterly reporting. The quarterly financials reported are necessary for all retail investors to know not only how the company is operating but also allows us to spot important abnormal information when filings are changed intentionally to hide operational errors or in cases of fraud. Filing twice a year will prolong this non-disclosure to retail investors, but not to insiders or those professional investors who have greater access. Therefore i ask that you continue to require public filings of all quarterly financial disclosures. 

Kind regards, 

Alastair Kay
