My name is Mary Conway, and I am currently the Director of Quality Management and Compliance at a well-respected community mental health center. I have actively participated in my various healthcare employers' 403b and other savings programs, and therefore in the stock market, throughout the last 43 years. 

I very much appreciate and value the quarterly reports of the companies that comprise my 403.B stock portfolio. I respectfully request that quarterly reports remain for those companies that are a part of the various U.S. Stock Exchanges. 

I also feel strongly that companies must actively incorporate transparency in their operations, and not act in a manner that would only benefit a minority of investors.
