                                 V.         CONCLUSION

         On May 5, 2026, The Securities and Exchange Commission issued proposed
amendments to allow companies to file semiannual reports on new Form 10-S in lieu of
quarterly reports on Form 10-Q to meet their interim reporting obligations. Also
proposed are changes to the financial statement requirements of Regulation S-X to
facilitate semiannual reporting and to simplify rules regarding the age of financial
statements. During 2026 alone, the disruptive imposition of tariffs, war in the mid-East,
outbreak of Ebola in central Africa, and accelerated changes in business dynamics due to
the influence of artificial intelligence (AI) and other transformative technologies argue in
favor of investors’ needing more information, faster, rather than less information
provided slowly. Accordingly, these comments argue that rapid technological advances
are causing the pace of business to move faster, not slower. As a result, I urge the
Commission to maintain a disclosure regime that encourages and requires more
disclosure, faster, not less transparency.
