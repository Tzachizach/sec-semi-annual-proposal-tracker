Please don’t change the SEC rules for companies reporting timelines. It would allow them to make their filings even more difficult for investors to track their earnings, price to sales ratios and the overall revenue for an entire six month period keeping investors in the dark about the overall health of the company. It’s bad enough that we have to depend on GAAP accounting principles which can be manipulated and now investors won’t be able to tell the full health of a company for 6 months? Please do not do this to the shareholders.
