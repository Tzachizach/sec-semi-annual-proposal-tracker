Taylor Kemp, Software Engineer, Quarterly reporting helps me make informed decisions on where my money is invested. If it is swapped to semi-annual I will likely try to move much of my money out of the current markets as lack of transparency would leave me with little trust in the very markets the SEC is supposedly meant to protect. We need more information not less. Companies already operate on half or annual planning at the lower levels so shifting the reporting to semi-annual won't help in creating longer term goaling. The purpose of the SEC should be transparency not growth or any other metric. Loosening reporting is exactly what leads to issues like the 2008 crash in real estate, Enron, Luckin Coffee, Evergrande etc. If anything the SEC should be pursuing much more stringent reporting requirements and should strive to set the global standard in transparency. This ruling if approved is only to help create a Ponzi scheme to enrich a few people with insider information at the cost of everyday Americans such as myself. The SEC should be protecting American pensions, retirement accounts & American's life savings not reducing transparency.
