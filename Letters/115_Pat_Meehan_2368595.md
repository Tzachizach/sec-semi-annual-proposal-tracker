This proposal will not survive the Supreme Court's ruling in Loper-Bright (2024) because agency heads, not receiving as much deference now, are supposed to come up with quantifiable justification for new rules. This proposal has theoretical, old, speculative, and data that any court will find insufficient. You give away your own game in footnote 332, admitting that you don't know what the impacts will be.
