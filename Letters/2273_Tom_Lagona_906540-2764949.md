Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Tom Lagona

I am against this propised change. Retail investors, and the public in general, should be getting MORE visibility into markets and companies on a more regular basis. Delaying and slowing down the flow of information will make it harder to make timely, informed, decisions. With how advanced we are, technology-wise, and with AI models becoming more reliable and accurate, I see no reason for this proposed change. Companies can easily get out this information in a timely manner and should be required to do so to keep as much transparency as possible within our markets.
