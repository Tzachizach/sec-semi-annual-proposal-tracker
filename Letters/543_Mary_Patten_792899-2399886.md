I usse quarterly reports to find companies that are increasing sales and earnings growth and to follow them for at lest 2 quarters to see if growth is continuing and use to make a decision to buy a stock.
Likewise, if a stock I own or am following has a decrease in growth or sales or in margin, I consider the reason and may decide to sell based on the first quarter results or minimally, keep a close watch on stock news to decide if I should sell.  Decision usually made before third quarter.
These quarterly reports provide me as much information as most professional investors.  If we go to semiannual/annual  reports, professionals will have more timely information than me because they have access/contact with companies that I don't have available to me.  

Don't take away the individual investor's data that is used to make timely decisions.
Deleting the quarterly report helps no one and hurts individual investors by hiding/concealing  actionable 
data for the individual investor
