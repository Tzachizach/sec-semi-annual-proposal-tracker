May 29, 2026

 
I do not support semiannual reporting. For the retail investor, company
information is limited, and market behavior opaque. Moving away from
quarterly reporting is absolutely the wrong direction. If anything,
company financial information is more obfuscated and less scrutinized
than ever, contributing to market manipulation, corruption,  and
bubbles. We need more transparency and reporting, not less!!! The
supposed benefits of this current proposal seem utterly trivial compared
with what would be lost. This is a bad and unnecessary idea. Please do
not implement this.

Sincerely,

Dr. Robert L. Jarecki, Jr.
