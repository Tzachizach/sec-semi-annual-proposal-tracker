I oppose allowing companies to opt out of quarterly reporting. There is no benefit to investors or stakeholders who are not corporate insiders. The savings to companies come at too high a cost to this country as it will enable fraud annd encourage insider trading. Be responsible to the public, not corporate lobbyists.
