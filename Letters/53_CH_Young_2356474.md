Subject: S7-2026-15: Webform Comments from CH Young
From: CH Young

This is a very bad idea. Singapore implemented semiannual reporting years ago. The result is company's disclosure became worse, transparency suffered, trading volume dwindled and valuations suffered. Now it is trying to encourage quarterly updates. Making it easy for companies is finding a wrong shortcut and a loser mentality. Companies are well aware of all obligations as public companies. They shouldn't list if they find it onerous. Don't come into the game if they can't cope. You don't get good players by making things easier. Also, it will skew the information dissemination to institutional investors who have direct access to key executives of companies, which is to the disadvantage of retail investors.
