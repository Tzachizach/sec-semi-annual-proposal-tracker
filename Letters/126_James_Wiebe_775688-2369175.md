Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: James Wiebe

If you're a public company, you should have to keep the public aware of the status of your company frequently enough so that the world of business can stay healthy and competitive. That's just common sense. I'm not saying this as a big investor but as an American, we all have a stake in the stability of our companies and the economy as a whole and if this were changed companies would be under less scrutiny and therefore less likely to behave according to the law.
