Do not change the quarterly reporting rules for companies. This would leave investors blind for months with no clear picture of what companies are doing. This will cripple the average investors who are the lifeblood of the markets. Stop this Now. It’s bad enough that we have to deal with GAAP accounting and now companies want to just not report for 6 months? No way is this good. Transparency goes out the window and the investors get blinded and have no idea what’s going on in the companies that they invest in.
