I believe quarterly reporting is one of the most important elements of publicly traded companies, they give information and insights into each company's financials in a reliable way. As an investor I believe that diluting that information to semianually just makes insider information more valuable and it would erode confidence in the stock market.

Please keep quarterly reporting mandatory.
