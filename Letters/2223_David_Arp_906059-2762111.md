Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: David Arp

I believe that companies should be required to submit quarterly reports.  In this era of having massive computers that can look at figures and make adjustments instantaneously, there is no reason to go to semi-annual reports where the public has less knowledge.  Additionally, some of these companies are the very same ones that build or participate in the use of data centers.  So they want data to improve on their positions but want to deny the public a much smaller amount of data on which the public uses to make investments in their individual futures.
