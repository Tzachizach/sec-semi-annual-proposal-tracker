Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Nikolay

Hello,

I strongly oppose proposed changes in reporting. As an investor I am heavily relying on quarterly reports in order to make proper investment decisions. Reducing the frequency of mandatory reporting will cause the companies to stop producing these reports and will significantly reduce market efficiency by allowing people involved in corporate insider trading to gain significantly more unfair advantage. The hypothetical burden of providing these reports using the frequency established for over a century is really close to zero and companies are actually benefitting from keeping their investors well informed and providing quarterly reports.

Thanks!
