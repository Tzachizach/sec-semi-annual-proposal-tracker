My name is R. Ryder and I would like to keep the rules for quarterly reporting mandatory instead of switching to semi-annual reporting (as it once was).

Quarterly reporting has helped improve the stability of the markets and helped the investors of Main Street. My portfolio had less issues after invoking the quarterly rule.

Rolling back regulations seems to have only hindered the stability of the market (see the last twenty years or so).

Please don’t switch to semi-annual reporting. My retirement is based mostly off my investments over the past several decades and I don’t want to have to weather another financial crisis that was easily preventable by regulations and more transparency.

Thank you for your time.


Sincerely,
R.
