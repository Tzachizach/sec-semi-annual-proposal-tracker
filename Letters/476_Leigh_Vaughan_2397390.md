Hi SEC, 
As a long term 80 year old, American citizen, and investor, I want to add my comment and objection to the recent consideration of only allowing USA public companies to file quarterly updates only every 6 months instead of every 3 month! 

Long-term and short-term investors rely on the continued, every 3 month transparency of public companies' requirement to update their quarterly accounting data, and future outlooks. Six months is too long without the required release of pertinent and trade-sensitive information! 

Please do not change the current requirement for Quarterly Reporting! Thank you, 

Leigh Vaughan
