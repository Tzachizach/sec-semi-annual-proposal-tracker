All corporations already produce monthly financial statements. It is
simply not that hard to consolidate three months of financial statements
into a quarterly report four times per year. Investors deserve to know
as much as they can about a companies success or lack of success as
often as possible. It will be a HUGE disaster to do away with quarterly
reporting. A bad CEO could avoid being fired for an extra six months if
quarterly reporting is abandoned. David Whittington - Hotel Controller
