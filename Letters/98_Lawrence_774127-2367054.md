Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Lawrence

Do not ease existing regulations to allow for semi-annual reporting instead of quarterly. Letting public companies report only twice a year is too little, and opens up greater windows into possible fraud. Frequent and regular updates provide significantly greater transparency, which is why the US market system is currently trusted by investors worldwide. Switching to semi-annual reporting dilutes this trust and harms the general public who rely on this information.
