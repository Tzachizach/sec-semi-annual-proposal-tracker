I do not want less information to come out from the companies I own shares of. Reducing quarterly statements to semi-annual would do just that. My understanding is a change like this would only increase volatility and keep an average investor like my self in the dark longer. In the grand scheme of things I don't buy the argument that it will save these publicly traded companies meaningful amounts of money by loosening these requirements and instead just increase the risk of insider trading.
