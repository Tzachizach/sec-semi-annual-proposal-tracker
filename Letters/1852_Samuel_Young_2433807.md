My name is Sam. I work in data analytics at a credit union in Virginia. I am an investor with retirement accounts as well as other stocks I have bought and sold. I am gravely concerned at this proposal to allow companies this level of leniency. There is far too great the opportunity for insider trading. With this time frame a half year can change a lot. Hide a lot. There is absolutely no inventive or positive outcome of this measure for me or any other investor for that matter. If this measure passes I will have lost all faith in the decision making of the SEC. It would bring into question the quality of our future. The only benefits will be for companies who feel the need to hide the reporting. The cost of quarterly vs bi annual is negligible at best. Do the right thing for your country. Do the right thing in general.
