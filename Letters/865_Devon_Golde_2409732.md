Affiliation: 

Jun. 1, 2026

 
I am opposed to the elimination of quarterly reporting. This rule undermines the SEC's core mission. It encourages corporations to operate with little transparency or oversight, both of which have repeatedly proven essential to protecting the broader economy and the public from unchecked corporate greed. If the SEC intends to overturn more than 50 years of established reporting standards, it should formally justify such a significant change and clearly demonstrate how reduced disclosure requirements would meaningfully benefit the public.
