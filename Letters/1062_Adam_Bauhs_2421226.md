Hi, 
My name is Adam Bauhs, and I am economics student at the University of Chicago. I'm writing to express my support for quarterly reporting. As we approach a world of increasing information collection and aggregation, I'm afraid of the widening disparity between sophisticated firms and small investors such as myself. Introducing of semi annual reporting would only widen this disparity, giving traders with access to proprietary data collection and insiders an even larger advantage over regular people. Purposely widening the information disparity would represent a perpetual transfer from ordinary people to the well connected. To senselessly blind ordinary investors would be a betrayal of the principles of free and fair markets, liberal democracy, and the American nation. 

Please retain the quarterly reporting schedule. 

Best, 
Adam
