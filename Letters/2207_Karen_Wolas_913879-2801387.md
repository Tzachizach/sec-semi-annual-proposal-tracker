Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Karen Wolas

I do not understand why companies would be allowed to semi-annual reporting versus quarterly.  All data is electronically available and can be collected and submitted quarterly as it currently is.  Delaying reports to every 6 months delays any financial decisions on a company's status and increases fraudulent activity if any.  Please keep quarterly reporting to promote transparency.
