Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Howard D. Tetrault

I am an individual investor. I depend on quarterly company reports for timely data to assess a company's financial health and make buy-sell-hold decisions on the company's stock. Standardized quarterly reporting allows me to track a company's earnings and debt along with other financial data to determine the health of the company. It is essential as part of my decision making process as an individual investor. 

Semi-annual reporting will not provide me with data in time to allow me to know if the company is making poor financial decisions and will not allow me to spot red flags in time to sell the stock. Quarterly reports  provide immediate insights into revenue, profit, losses, and expenses, showing if a company is meeting its goals or experiencing issues. Quarterly tracking allows me to see trends that can identify if the company is having any financial issues. 

Please do not change the reporting period of company financial data to semi-annual from the current quarterly reporting period.
