Manufacturing Manager 
My 401k and investments are already too much at the mercy of people whom I have to put my trust in to make sure I can retire. To make the reporting structure of the companies my retirement is invested in have less transparency is not good for me or anyone else working to provide goods and services to the American people. Keep the quarterly reporting for the betterment of the rest of us, not for the benefit of those who are already well off enough to not be hurt by the change.
