Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Colene Walden

As a longtime member of an NAIC investment club, I am opposed to any change in company reporting regulations.  Without the transparancy of regular company reports, retail investors are at a disadvantage in making investment decisions.  The experience I have gained from my club membership has contributed to a comfortable financial retirement.  I believe that all should have access to the learning opportunity that club participation affords; to that end government should continue to mandate quarterly company disclosure.
