Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Shirley Williams

I suggest making the first report of the year for four months which gives companies an extra month to recover from annual and year end audits. Then do a report after 7 months instead of semi-annual reports. Then another report after 10 months. This will give them only two months to add for annual reports and to calculate yearly taxes or remaining taxes due, and this should make everything easier at year-end.
