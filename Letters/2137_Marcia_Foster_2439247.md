Jun. 7, 2026

 
I am concerned about reducing reporting to semi-annual. In a time when things are moving so quickly, I want more information, not less. Individuals are already at a disadvantage, not knowing laws and finance as the experts do. Making it optional to comply with quarterly reporting puts the burden on those with fewer resources and, eventually the “option” is likely to disappear.

Marcia Foster
