Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Joseph Adair

I am AGAINST the proposed change that would allow companies that issue stock to submit financial reports on a semi-annual basis.

The quarterly reports allow individuals, with limited resources, to review the quarterly data to determine if a Company stock should be bought or sold.

This would be like returning to the days before computers when you had to track the stock's price using the newspaper and when I had to go to the library archives section to see how a Company was performing.
