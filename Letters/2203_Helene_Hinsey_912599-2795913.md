Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Helene Hinsey

I am strongly against reducing the quarterly reporting requirement to semi-annual or annual reports. This reduces the transparency between publically traded companies and their investors and could increase investors risks significantly. This is especially true for investments in 401ks, IRAs and other retirement plans where investors abd their advisors depend on these reports to make informed decisions. Thank you.
