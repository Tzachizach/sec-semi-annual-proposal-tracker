Hi, my name is Ira Gottlieb and I worked in leadership roles for public companies for more than 20 years. The requirement to report financial results quarterly helped provide the rigor and discipline to run the business efficiently and with a sense of urgency. I believe moving to a bi-annual reporting is a bad idea, and gives companies too much room to let their discipline and compliance lapse - while giving investors less visibility into the performance of the companies they are invested in. Please reconsider this decision.
