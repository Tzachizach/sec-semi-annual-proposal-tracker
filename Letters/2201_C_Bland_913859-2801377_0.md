Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: C. Bland

To the SEC Chairman, 
I am a former Series 7 and 9/10 professional w 30 years in the industry. I am stunned that this rule change is even being considered. We live in a world where fraud and corruption are rampant. Are we going to just succumb to the trend by giving the top 5% the opportunity to transact business under the cloak of darkness for 6 months?  The enactment of this rule will roll us back to 1929. It's all well and good until the middle class is wiped out and then who will buy the products and services these publicly traded corporations are selling?  Try to be the hard regulatory agency I had to deal with and whom I had respect for. Do not approve this scam of a rule. It will have devastating market repercussions down the line. 
Respectfully, C Bland
