Jun. 7, 2026

 
Keeping corporate reporting quarterly is the absolute minimum that should be required for publicly-traded companies. Even quarterly gives a lot of opportunity to hide bad information from investors; semi-annual reporting allows far too much opportunity for company leadership to take risks with investors' money for too long, or to take other actions of which investors and the public may not be aware as soon as they need to in order to take action. Reducing the accountability of people and institutions entrusted with others' resources is only a good thing when you have something to hide.
