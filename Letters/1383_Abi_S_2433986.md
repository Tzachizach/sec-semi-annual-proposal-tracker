Quarterly reporting has been the standard for a reason. There is no reason and no excuse for cutting back on reporting in the modern era when access to timely information is easier, and more critical, than ever. This change would not offer relief to companies who have been reporting at this frequency for decades but it would put those who rely on accurate and timely reports at a disadvantage. 

Thank you for your consideration.
