I am writing to express my strong opposition to the proposal of changing the 10-Q reporting requirement from a quarterly to a semi-annual basis. 

As an individual investor with a portfolio of over 50 companies, I rely heavily on the data provided in quarterly filings to make informed decisions on whether to hold, buy, or sell my positions. Frequent financial reporting is essential to ensuring that the numbers remain reliable and transparent. Reducing the frequency of these reports would significantly limit my ability to monitor my investments and react to financial changes in a timely manner. 

I believe that maintaining quarterly 10-Q filings is vital for investor protection and market integrity. Thank you for your time and for considering my perspective on this matter. 

Best regards, 

Gloria Gunn
