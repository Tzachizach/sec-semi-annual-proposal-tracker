Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Beverly A. Bryant

I strongly oppose the proposed rule and form amendments that would give public companies the option of filing semiannual reports in lieu of quarterly reports to meet their interim reporting obligations under the federal securities laws.  In today's technological world, most companies are capable of generating reports within a very short window of time, therefore giving public companies the option of reporting semiannually is unacceptable.  Complete transparency must be required.  The proposed ruling needs to be changed from semiannual to MONTHLY.
