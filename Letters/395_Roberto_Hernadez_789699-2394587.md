Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Roberto Hernadez

I as an American Citizen and Investor am 100% against this proposal. This dramatic reduction in transparency gives company insiders a massive informational advantage over ordinary shareholders, who would have far fewer data points to detect deteriorating financials, accounting irregularities, or strategic missteps before they cause serious losses. Far from reducing meaningful compliance burden, since the heavy lifting of auditing, internal controls, and financial statement preparation happens on a rolling basis regardless of reporting frequency. The rule primarily serves to obscure corporate performance, undermining the core investor protection mission the SEC was created to fulfill.
