Affiliation: 

Jun. 1, 2026

 
I write in opposition to the proposed shift to semiannual reporting. This is not going to help retail in any way. And isn’t retail just as important as the firms that commit fraud every day? You have Citadel that operates a hedge fund and also has a market maker arm. That’s an inherent problem and conflict of interest. So with this rule they only have to cook the books twice a year instead of four times a year. This is going to be disastrous if this passes.
