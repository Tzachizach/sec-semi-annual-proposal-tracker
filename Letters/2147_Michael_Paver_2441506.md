To the deciding members at the SEC

As a married couple approaching retirement age, our investments will soon be our only source of income, and having as much information as possible to make informed investment choices is paramount. Loosening reporting regulations on publicly traded corporations and allowing for less transparency and accountability to shareholders is a horrible idea on principle. It’s also been tried before and led to market devaluation. Leave this terrible idea where it belongs, in the dustbin of history, and keep markets transparent and strong!
