Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: James Kleen

I do not support the proposed rule change referenced in S7-2026-15.

The potential for increased risk and fraud to investors substantially outweighs any benefits cited in support of extending the reporting frequency from quarterly to semi-annual (every six months). Reducing the frequency of required disclosures would weaken transparency and investor protections, which could ultimately harm market integrity and public confidence.
I urge the Commission to retain the current quarterly reporting requirements.
