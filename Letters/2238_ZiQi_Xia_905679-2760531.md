Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: ZiQi Xia

Dear U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission,I am submitting a formal complaint regarding potential regulatory changes that would reduce public company financial disclosures from quarterly to semi-annually. This change directly threatens investor protection . As a 17-year-old investor, I believe that if this policy is passed, it will make companies far less transparent, allowing them to hide crucial information. It would also give corporate insiders a much larger window to trade on material, non-public information, creating an extremely unfair advantage over ordinary traders like me. Furthermore, waiting half a year for financial updates will cause extreme market volatility when the data is finally released. This risks causing stocks to either skyrocket or be completely wiped out, as markets will be forced to price in six months of unknown variables all at once.v To ensure fair, orderly, and efficient markets, I implore the SEC to protect individual investors like me by maintaining the mandatory quarterly reporting framework.
