Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Michael Turyn

Corporate fraud and malfeasance are already out-of-control under current circumstances; doubling the period for reporting can only increase them. For many running them, a corporation is a tool for evading personal responsibility.

As the wisest Founder, Benjamin Franklin, observed, all property beyond some small holding is the creation of society and subject to the just needs of society, and this is especially so in the case of public corporations. Artificial personhood and limited liability are unknown in the State of Nature, they are our creations, and—taking a lesson from fictional case of Baron Frankenstein—we ought to keep a keen eye on the beings we create and to whom we have given great power.
