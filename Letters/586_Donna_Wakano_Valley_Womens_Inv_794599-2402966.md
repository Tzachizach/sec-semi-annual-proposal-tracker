Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Donna Wakano - Valley Womens Investment Club

I have recently become aware of the SEC's intent to cut quarterly reports in half. Quarterly reports are vital to members of my investment club. We depend on standardized and accurate quarterly information to make informed group decisions. Many members are also individual investors who deem these reports as vital. Additionally, members assigned to monitor individual stocks held within our portfolio find the quarterly reports a necessary tool for club decisions. Thank you for your attention in this matter.
