I believe quarterly reports are necessary to ensure accountability and transparency. Six months is too long to wait for an update. And given the power of technology, it should be easy for companies to comply with quarterly requirements. Frankly, it's their job and they need to do it. 

There is danger in a bad actor being able to use a longer reporting period to conceal fundamental problems.
