We need more transparency, not less. This rule does not benefit investors nor does it help companies. To make informed investments in solid companies, investors need demonstrable facts. To minimize investment by emotion and manipuation, companies must continue to provide the facts on a quarterly basis. The world is changing too fast to wait 6 months for information. Talk about putting the pig in the poke! This measure will cause further erosion of faith in American business. The dangers far outweigh any minimal benefit.
