Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Hope M. Anderson

I oppose File No. S7-2026-15. Reducing corporate financial reporting from quarterly to semi-annually destroys market transparency and directly harms everyday American investors. Individual investors need timely, reliable quarterly data to protect our savings and make informed decisions. Please withdraw this proposal and maintain the current quarterly reporting framework.
