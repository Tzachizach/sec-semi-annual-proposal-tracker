Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Andrew Dempsey

Investors deserve more transparency, not less. Requiring companies to report quarterly results allows investors to monitor patterns and keep a pulse on how the company is performing. Extending the reporting period will just erode investor confidence and lead to even more share price volatility on announcement days.
