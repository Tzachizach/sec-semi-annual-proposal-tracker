Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Michelle C.

I disagree with this proposed change.

Quarterly reporting was put into place to ensure a more transparent outlook on publicly traded companies. Given the majority of the American people are now responsible for managing their own financial and retirement portfolios - it is inappropriate to limit access to the performance of the companies in which they invested and own.
