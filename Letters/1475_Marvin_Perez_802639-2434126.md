Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Marvin Perez

This proposed ruling would be a disaster for small retail investors like me that perform their due diligence with quarterly earning reports. Having less data from companies (twice a year vs 4 times a year) would be a great Public disservice for small retail investors like myself and would only benefit insiders with private information that would currently be made public on a quarterly basis. Please reject this new ruling and uphold the current quarterly reporting basis.
