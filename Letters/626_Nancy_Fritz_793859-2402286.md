Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Nancy Fritz

It is my opinion that the reporting requirement timeline should not change for business reporting. Investors need to see current information in order to make informed decisions. Without this information investors will be less likely to invest in a timely manner. The figures are needed, but maybe the process and paperwork could be streamlined. But to do away with the reporting will not be beneficial in the long run.
