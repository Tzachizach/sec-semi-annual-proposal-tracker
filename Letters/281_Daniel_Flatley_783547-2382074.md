Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Daniel Flatley

I am absolutely against discontinuing quarterly reports, believing that investors should have as much information as is reasonably possible to provide without overburdening reporting companies. In that vein, I would suggest optional, significantly abbreviated, interim monthly reports combined with Congressionally approved shareholder strike suit immunization to encourage disclosure.
