Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Chase Hardy

It is essential that organizations continue to release quarterly reports and are not allowed to release reports semiannually. The information that comes from those reports is crucial for investor decision making, and one report every six months is not enough. 
Please protect the investor community by not allowing organizations to release reports semiannually. 
I oppose this proposed rule and urge the Commission not to adopt it.
