Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Ged Cleary

Less information to interested parties only assists the unscrupulous. One who wishes to hide an issue does not want information leaking out to those outside the inner sanctum. Information shared in any disclosure can be massaged to convey any purpose but if management has a directive to be honest, accurate and impartial then that should at the very least, ensure clarity of information to owners. The more often this information is disclosed should keep everyone on their best behaviour.
