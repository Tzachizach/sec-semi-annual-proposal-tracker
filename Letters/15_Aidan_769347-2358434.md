# Letter 15 — Aidan

- **Date:** 2026-05-06
- **Role/Affiliation:** Individual
- **Stance:** Oppose
- **Source:** https://www.sec.gov/comments/S7-2026-15/s7202615-769347-2358434.html

---

Quarterly reporting creates a public good for individual and professional investors. What would have happened if Enron only had to cook their books semiannually? What would have happened during the financial crisis? Public investors deserve timely updates on public companies. This will widen the knowledge gap between individuals and institutional investors and reduce accountability for public companies. The longer period of time these financial statements cover, the more unintelligible the information will become.
