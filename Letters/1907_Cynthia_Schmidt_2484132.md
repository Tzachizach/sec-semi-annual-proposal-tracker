Keep the quarterly reporting requirement. It definitely provides the technical and fiscal information required for investing by smaller retail investors. The information I can research quarterly definitely assists me in my decision making. Cutting the reporting back to semi-annually will provide the reporting business with excuses to misstate their reporting and activity. The advent of computer usage makes it extremely easy for PUBLIC TRADED companies to report their financial conditions. I want to be able to keep up with large institutional investors and the information they receive. They have access to corporate management that I do not.
