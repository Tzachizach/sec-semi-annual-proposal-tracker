Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: RK Cradit

I am writing to express my opposition to Proposed Rule S7-2026-15 which would allow companies to elect semiannual reporting, as opposed to the currently required quarterly reporting requirement. It is my assertion that quarterly reporting provides valuable information for individual investors like me when considering investment decisions. It is my hope that quarterly reporting continues to be the requirement.
