Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Craig Cutler

The purpose of quarterly reporting is not for the company benefit, it is for the investors.    Proving the investor less information so the company can save money or remove pressure is the opposite of beneficial governance.   We have had many examples of companies misrepresenting their financial performance.  It will be much easier to mislead and much harder for an investor to compare companies with half the information.   Thiis would be an undeserving gift to corporations at investor expense
