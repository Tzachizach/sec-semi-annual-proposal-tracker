Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Gregory Valentine

It's important that companies provide investors with regular quarterly reports. The access to this information should be required and easily accessible for all investors, no matter the size of the investment. I have personally used this information in the past to decide whether or not to buy, sell, or hold a financial product and allowing companies to delay these reports would negatively affect my ability to participate in the stock market. Also, I have never worked for a company where providing this information to investors was a burden, and if it was it would be an indication of financial negligence.
