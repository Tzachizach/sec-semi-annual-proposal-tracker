Jun. 7, 2026

 
Quarterly is the MINIMUM we, as investors, should expect to get earnings reports from businesses we choose to invest in.

It’s absurd to believe that I (or any other common investor) would continue investing in ANY corporation if it came with the stipulation that we cannot get any real information of what their finances look like except for twice a year. In that extra time, RADICAL changes can and do occur causing the investors to potentially lose a great deal of money, while the company, well knowing that its risk has increased can just continue to take advantage of the investment we’ve made in its future without any responsibility.

This is absolutely unacceptable. We need to help the individuals that keep our country running and advancing, not the corporations who only care about their own profits.

Please do NOT support this proposed change.

L Lindenbaum
Associate Professor of Anesthesiology and Critical Care Medicine
