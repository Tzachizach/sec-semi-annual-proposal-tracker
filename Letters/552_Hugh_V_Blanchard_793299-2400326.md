Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Hugh V Blanchard

I strongly oppose the proposal to reduce SEC reporting to only twice a year.  My family's investment portfolio  depends on timely and accurate information regarding the financial health of a company as a potential investment.  Cutting the SEC filing to only twice annually leaves investors with untimely information that could be several months out of date. This could result in bad decision being made as to the possible performance of an investment.  This is unacceptable.  Please retain the quarterly reporting requirement and take care of U.S. citizens who depend on these reports for timely and accurate information.
