Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Meagan

I believe that the information provided in quarterly reporting not only offers protections to consumers, employees, and investors, but also encourages a more competitive market. It allows for informed business decision making. Additionally, as someone who works in market research/data analytics, moving to semi-annual reporting will have real consequences on industries that use 10Q reports as a reference for their research, products, or decisions. The cost savings are minimal for a policy change that withholds information from the public and creates volatility and insecurity in the market. We have a right to make informed personal and professional decisions more than twice a year. Thank you for the consideration of my comment.
