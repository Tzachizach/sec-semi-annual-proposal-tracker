Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Jose Sainz

I am writing to voice my displeasure with the new reporting period of 6 months. While I initially supported it after more research I decided that we simply cannot trust companies to do the right thing and not to be scandalous and hide whatever they can from the public. If I've learned anything so far in my millennial life it's that companies always do the wrong thing or whatever enriches top executives first and leaves everyone else holding the bag. Although quarterly reports probably cause more events to happen like layoffs I just don't think companies will do the ethical thing most of the time and not hide problems for longer or do insider trading. So keep the system how it is.
