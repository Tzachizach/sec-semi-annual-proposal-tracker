My name is Hope Cook. I am a retired nurse in Houston, Texas. 
As an investor in the stock market I think that quarterly reporting to the SEC is a significant safeguard tor investors. There seems to be a trend towards less open reporting from many quarters. I don't think that is a good thing. 
I believe decreased reporting increases the chances for the market to be controlled/manipulated by those with large amounts of money and private information sources to their benefit and to the detriment of the rest of us. The world moves quickly these days. Six month reporting leaves a significant gap where many things can happen before the public is aware. 
Many people who have investments in the stock market are putting away money for their retirement. And with talk of "privatizing" Social Security that would surely increase. This makes this issue important to us all. 
It is rarely a good thing to have less information. Cutting costs should not be at the expense of the public. 

I sincerely plead for you to stop SEC Proposal S7-2026-15 and continue with quarterly reporting.
