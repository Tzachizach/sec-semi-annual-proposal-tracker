Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Todd Hansen

I believe in free markets. Let the market decide. I'm fine with less frequent reporting. It should be up to the company. If an investor wants quarterly reporting, they can choose to invest in companies that voluntarily issue quarterly reports. If those companies attract more capital, perhaps more companies will voluntarily inform their investors on a quarterly or even monthly basis. As an adult investor, I can pick where I invest my money. I don't need a government to protect me. The marketplace will protect me.
Thank you.
