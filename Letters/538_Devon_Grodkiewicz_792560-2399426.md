Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Devon Grodkiewicz

Quarterly reporting requirements are critical for investor knowledge when making evaluations of a public equity. When conducting analysis, feature development requires robust longitudinal time series data. This decision would reduce the number of records available for analysis by 50% and is a terrible idea.

I repeat: this is a terrible idea and our governance and data access structures need to be more robust, not less robust.  Please don't do this.
