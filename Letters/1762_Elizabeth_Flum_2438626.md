I am writing in support of keeping the quarterly reporting rule for public corporations. As a private citizen who has been investing in the US stock market with my 401K for decades I need timely information about the performance of my holdings. Corporations need to be held accountable for their business decisions, and investors deserve timely information about their hard-earned savings. The SEC proposal allowing business "to go dark" for six months at a time is just a shady idea that endangers the interests of all investors - Don't fall for it!
