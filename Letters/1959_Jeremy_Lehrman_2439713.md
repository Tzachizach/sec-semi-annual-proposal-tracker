The "financial burden" imposed by quarterly reporting is vastly overstated, as so much of corporate reporting is now automated. Do NOT go back to semi-annual reporting. The last thing any investor wants is LESS transparency into the finances of publicly traded companies. Please stick with a system that has, by-and-large, worked for more than 50 years.
