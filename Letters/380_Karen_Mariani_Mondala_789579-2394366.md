Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Karen Mariani-Mondala

Please reconsider your plan to require companies to file financial reports to you only twice a year. This would undermine the financial transparency we rely on when deciding whether to add to our current stock holdings or whether to invest at all. Every quarter we review the SEC financial fillings  for the companies in our stock club portfolio which we began in 1998. We have managed to grow that self managed portfolio to over a million dollars! It would not have been possible without having access to good financial records that were submitted to the SEC. We count on these for accuracy and transparency! Please, do not weaken the financial oversight for our country by removing the requirement that companies report quarterly earnings to The Security and Exchange Commission!!! We small retired investors are relying on you!!! Thank you for the fine financial oversight service you have provided for years. Please continue your important contribution to our country's financial integrity and its citizens wealth building opportunity!!!
