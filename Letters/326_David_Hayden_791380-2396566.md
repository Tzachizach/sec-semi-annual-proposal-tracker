Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: David Hayden

As technology gets better and cheaper, there's no reason to permit less frequent financial reporting. Heck, companies could provide MORE frequent reporting with the same or less effort than 10 years ago. There is no reason to allow companies to report every 6 months.

As an individual investor, I rely strongly on the SEC filings to get "real" data instead of the spin that so many companies give in their press releases. Please hold corporations to the standards that we've used for decades.
