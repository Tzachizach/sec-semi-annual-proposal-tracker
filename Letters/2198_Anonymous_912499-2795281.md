Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Anonymous

My husband and I oppose semiannual statements. We want quarterly, even monthly, statements mailed to us. As seniors we are not trusting or savvy regarding the internet, online banking, identifying fake sites, spoof emails and the constant online burden to prove who we are. Too much time between statements gives companies the ability to do risky finances without the knowledge of investors. I don't trust the government to adequately police companies. It didn't go well in the past, I don't expect it today.
