Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Heather Disselkoen

Transparency requires financial information to be shared in a timely manner. Quarterly reporting is the minimum acceptable reporting period to ensure accountability and avoid abuse of the system. I urge you to maintain quarterly reporting as weakening this requirement will undermine the public's trust in your agency during an economic period that requires steady and reliable assurances.
