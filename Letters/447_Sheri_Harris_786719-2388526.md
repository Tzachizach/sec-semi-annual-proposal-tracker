This quarterly snapshot, while already providing stale data, gives an opportunity to observe trends, both positive and negative, to influence investor decisions. Reducing the periodicity to semiannual increases investor risk with investor decisions being based on very stale performance data because out of date performance information would permit significant trends to be obscured.
