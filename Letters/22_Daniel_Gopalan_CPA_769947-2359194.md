# Letter 22 — Daniel Gopalan, CPA

- **Date:** 2026-05-06
- **Role/Affiliation:** CPA
- **Stance:** Oppose
- **Source:** https://www.sec.gov/comments/S7-2026-15/s7202615-769947-2359194.html

I am against removing quarterly reporting. The financial markets move on information. Less information means less price discovery. This will reduce the efficiency of the markets and open the door for more accounting fraud to fester in the markets.
