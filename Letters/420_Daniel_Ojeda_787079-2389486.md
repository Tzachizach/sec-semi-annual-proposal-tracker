Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Daniel Ojeda

As an investor information about publicly traded companies must be provided transparently so normal investors have a window into the companies in which they invest. This modified ruling servers to obscure information, allow for more insider trading and erode trust in institutions. Entities that do not wish to be beholden to investors should remain private.
