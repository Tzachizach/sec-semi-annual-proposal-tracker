I do not support a 6 month investor reporting structure for the following reasons. (1) Data changes too frequently as it is. I rely on quarterly information to make decisions, (2) longer gaps create an unfair advantage for corporate insiders, (3) "optional" doesn't fix the problem because companies that opt in are often the ones whose investors most need frequent updates, (4) the savings to companies aren't worth the cost to me.
