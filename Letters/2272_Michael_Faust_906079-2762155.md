Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Michael Faust

I understand that a proposal is now on the table to allow companies to elect to reduce the number of public-company reports from four times a year to just two. I think that's a mistake! As a professional business broker for over 20 years, I recognize the importance of timely interim financial reporting. Quarterly reporting by public companies should remain the minimum.
