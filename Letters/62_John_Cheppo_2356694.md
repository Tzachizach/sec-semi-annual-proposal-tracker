Subject: S7-2026-15: Webform Comments from John Cheppo
From: John Cheppo

Dear SEC,
This is an awful idea. I have worked in companies for decades and have now retired. This would not change any long term behaviors, and simply just increase non-transparency and further harm individual investors ability to identify, assess and mitigate risks. Quarterly reporting is a bedrock of making companies try to do the right thing, it's not a problem. I cannot even believe this is up for consideration. Do not permit this please.
