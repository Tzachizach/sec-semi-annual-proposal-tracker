Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: C. Yu

I am an American with my retirement invested in the stock market. I am also a retired attorney with exposure to securities regulation in my past practice. As someone on "both sides" of this type of rule-making, I have absolutely no doubt that this rule would be absolutely terrible for both the market and individual investors. It would incentivize insider trading and securities fraud.

As the famous saying goes, sunshine is the best disinfectant. This rule change casts us all back into the Dark Ages of regulation and will only give us a diseased securities market.
