Subject: File No. S7-2026-15

I strongly oppose the idea of semi-annual reporting without requiring audit certification and the inclusion of legal jeopardy attached to management. It would be rife with obfuscations, making it impossible for investment managers to accurately assess the company. Surely AI will streamline the creation of 10-Qs and the analysis of same for all parties.
Thank you for your consideration of these concerns.
Sincerely,
Christi Powell, CFP
