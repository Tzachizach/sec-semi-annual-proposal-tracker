Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Paul Kalenak

I am against changing the reporting period for corporations from 3 months to 6 months. Quarterly financial reporting holds companies accountable to their shareholders allowing informed investment decisions by them. Various government statistics are reported monthly. I don't think it's difficult for companies to continue reporting quarterly.
