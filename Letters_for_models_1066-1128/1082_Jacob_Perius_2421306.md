SEC regulators and officials,

Access to quarterly financial documents is imperative for Americans and those we entrust our capital with to make informed investment decisions.

This reporting cadence also forces a level of transparency and accountability for those running public companies.

These same companies dominate industries and hold ever-growing influence over our lives. It is of increasing importance that these companies provide insights into their activities to regulators and policymakers, especially as the rate of technological change is much greater than ever before.

Let’s maintain the integrity of our financial markets! Enforce continued quarterly reporting.

Respectfully,
