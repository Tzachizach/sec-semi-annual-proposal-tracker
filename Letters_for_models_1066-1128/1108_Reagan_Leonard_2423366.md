My name is Reagan. I live in South Carolina and I work as a web developer. This proposal to allow companies to report their financials every SIX MONTHS is insane to me. Personally, I am not the day-trading type. I prefer auto-investing into broad low-cost index funds and ETFs. However, even for a person like me who doesn't necessarily read companies' quarterly reports and make investment decisions based on them, this is bad news. It gives companies a longer leash to do irresponsible things without investors' and stakeholders' knowledge. To me, this is yet another example of our government telling the public, "We're going to change the law so that corporations can financially benefit the already-wealthy class (who are blatantly and unashamedly insider trading) MORE while they will benefit you, the working class, less". This will allow the wealth gap between normal US citizens and the 1% to grow even larger because it holds normal people at even further arms length while those rules do not apply to the ultra wealthy. ...But I bet you guys at the SEC already know all this, don't you?
DO NOT DO THIS. Companies should still have to file earnings reports QUARTERLY.
