Financial reporting needs to be timely, consistent, and accurate. Timely access to publicly available reporting is a fundamental part of investment profiling. Retail investors are already at a major disadvantage and giving further leeway to potential insider trading will further erode confidence in American markets. Public companies will always push for more leeway to take on risk. It is a regulator's duty to push back on behalf of shareholders, employees, and consumers. Lack of oversight inevitably leads to irreparable damage to the longevity of an organization and the economy by extension. Well-meaning or not, idiots will abuse their position, risk what isn't theirs, and lose it all.
