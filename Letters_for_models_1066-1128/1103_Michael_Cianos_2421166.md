I hope that the SEC can appreciate the role that it has to play in building a healthy society and economy for everyone. Corporations have shown time and time again that they do not give a damn about the broader society they are embedded within, and allowing for even more secrecy benefits no one but the privileged few on the inside. For decades, they have made the rules that they get to play by - and look where it's gotten us. Sky high stockmarket but most people struggling just to get by. This is the system you're complicit in creating. I have little faith in your ability to see that anymore, but providing yet another avenue for a handful of people to protect themselves and get richer while everyone else flies blind is so clearly asinine. But the mere fact that I even need to write this comment means we're too far gone. 

In a world where it is becoming harder by the day to find real information about what is going on - don't make it even harder. 

- Mike
