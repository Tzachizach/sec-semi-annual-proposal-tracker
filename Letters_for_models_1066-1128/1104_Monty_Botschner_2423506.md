Please maintain the quarterly reporting requirements of financial results for public companies. If they are switched to 6 month reporting intervals, this will negatively affect me as a retail trader. I rely on the quarterly reports for my investing strategy. Inside traders will benefit from the longer gap, but I would be put at a disadvantage as a retail trader. The deck is already stacked against the retail trader and this would just add to their disadvantage. The minimal savings to the companies are not worth the disadvantage this would cause to me. 

I am asking that the quarterly reporting of financial of public companies be maintained.
