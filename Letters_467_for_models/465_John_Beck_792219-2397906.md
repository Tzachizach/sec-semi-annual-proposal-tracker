Subject: File Number S7-2026-15
I am an individual investor. I rely heavily on the quarterly reported information from the public companies in which I have holdings or about which I am studying to determine if I should acquire their stock. Getting that information only twice a year would severely limit my knowledge of those companies. Many small and medium size companies do not have analyst coverage by any of the major financial data organizations such as Value Line and Morningstar.
Please consider the smaller individual investor and our limited access to information about company performance upon which we rely to make solid investment decisions.
