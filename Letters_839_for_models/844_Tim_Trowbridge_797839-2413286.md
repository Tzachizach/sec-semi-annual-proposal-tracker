# Letter 844 — Tim Trowbridge

- **Date:** 2026-05-27

Subject: File No. S7-2026-15
From: Tim Trowbridge

I believe that reducing corporate reporting requirements to semi-annually will deprive investors of timely, critical information, regarding the companies in which they have invested. Given the rapidity at which markets move in our global environment it is imperative that the owners of corporations, the shareholders, are provided timely financial performance information and the reports of the managers. Semi-annual reporting will increase uncertainty about company performance and thus increase volatility of individual stocks and the markets as a whole. Please reject  S7-2026-15.
