I am opposed to the change to six month reporting primarily due to it providing a greater opportunity for insiders to trade and benefit from it. Perhaps the SEC should spend its time on trying to reduce insider trading, both by corporate insiders and politicians. I suggest all Federal office holders be required to place their investments in trusts over which they have no direct control regarding investment decisions.

Christopher Janik
