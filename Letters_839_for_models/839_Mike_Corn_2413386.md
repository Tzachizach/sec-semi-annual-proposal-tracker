Quarterly corporate reporting is important, for regulators and investors. Without quarterly reporting, a wide window is made available for all things troublesome. Investors and regulators both will not have current data upon which to measure. Besides, virtually every corporation will continue to compile the quarterly data, so it will not be an extra burden. In short, we need quarterly reports. Reject the change and keep quarterly reporting rules.

Mike Corn
Hays, Kansas.
